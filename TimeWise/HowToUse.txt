TimeWise iOS App - How to Use Guide

Introduction

Welcome to TimeWise! This user guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to use the TimeWise app to manage your party setup and guest management. TimeWise is designed to help you organize and plan your parties effectively. Let's get started!


Table of Contents

1. Installation
2. Account Setup
3. Party Setup
4. Managing Guests
5. Settings


1. Installation

To install the TimeWise app, follow these simple steps:

1. Open the App Store or Google Play Store on your mobile device.
2. Search for "TimeWise" in the search bar.
3. Locate the TimeWise app in the search results.
4. Tap the "Install" or "Get" button to download and install the app.
5. Once the installation is complete, you can find the TimeWise app on your home screen.


2. Account Setup

Before you can start using the TimeWise app, you need to set up your account. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Launch the TimeWise app from your home screen.
2. You will be prompted to create an account or log in.
3. If you're a new user, tap on "Create Account" and provide the necessary information, such as your name, email address, and password. If you already have an account, tap on "Log In" and enter your credentials.
4. Once you've entered the required information, tap on "Sign Up" or "Log In" to create your account or log in to your existing account.


3. Party Setup

TimeWise allows you to easily set up your parties and manage the details. Follow these steps to set up a party:

1. From the main menu, tap on "Party Setup."
2. You will be presented with a form to enter party details.
3. Enter the party location by either typing it in the "Party Location" text field or tapping on the "GET" button to automatically fetch your current location.
4. Select the date, start time, and end time of the party using the provided date and time pickers.
5. Set the GHB (Gamma-Hydroxybutyric Acid) limit by adjusting the stepper control. A warning will be displayed if the limit exceeds 1.5 ml.
6. If there are any additional substances, toggle the respective switches to include them in the party setup.
7. Once you have entered all the necessary information, tap on the "Save Party Setup" button to save your party details.


4. Managing Guests

TimeWise allows you to efficiently manage your party guests. Follow these steps to manage your guests:

1. From the main menu, tap on "Guest Management."
2. You will be presented with a list of your party guests.
3. To add a new guest, tap on the "+" button and enter the guest's name and contact information.
4. To edit a guest's information, tap on the guest's name in the list and make the necessary changes.
5. To remove a guest from the list, swipe left on the guest's name and tap on the "Delete" button.
6. You can also search for specific guests using the search bar at the top of the guest list.


5. Settings

TimeWise provides various settings options to customize your app experience. Follow these steps to access the app settings:

1. From the main menu, tap on the "Settings" button.
2. You will be presented with a list of available settings.
3. Tap on each setting to modify its respective options.
4. Make the desired changes and navigate back to the previous screen to save your settings.


Conclusion

Congratulations! You have now learned how to use the TimeWise app to manage your party setup and guest management. Make the most of TimeWise to plan and organize your parties effectively. If you have any further questions or need assistance, feel free to consult the in-app help or contact our support team. Enjoy your party planning with TimeWise!
